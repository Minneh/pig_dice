How to Play

The key to winning at Pig Dice is knowing when to stop rolling and end your turn. It's an exercise in deciding between gut instinct and greed.


Step 1: Roll one die for your first turn.

Step 2: A roll of two, three, four, five, or six gains one the respective points. If you roll a one, you lose all the points you accumulated during that turn, and you lose your turn.

Step 3: One can choose to "hold" which saves the points they have so far and shifts the turn to the other player.

Step 4: Reach 100 points first to win the game!
